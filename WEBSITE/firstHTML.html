Randomness

According to Ramsey theory, pure randomness is impossible, especially for large structures. Mathematician Theodore Motzkin suggested that "while disorder is more probable in general, complete disorder is impossible". Misunderstanding this can lead to numerous conspiracy theories.
Cristian S. Calude stated that "given the impossibility of true randomness, the effort is directed towards studying degrees of randomness".

It can be proven that there is infinite hierarchy
(in terms of quality or strength) of forms of randomness.
